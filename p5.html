SIX-DIRECTIONAL CONTROLLER

HackMIT Hardware Hackathon Winner

What?

  • Created an inutuitive 3-D controller for the entertainment track at HackMIT
  • Developed custon 3-D Snake game to work seamlessly with the controller

How?

  • Used potentiometers to measure movement of the central control piece
  • SLA printed a custom frame to house electronics and mount control mechanism
  • Wrote custom firmware to convert analog potentiometer signals into digital inputs readable by a computer

Results

  • Created a fully functional 3-D controller that could easily be used by a player without previous gaming experience
  • Won HackMIT for best hardware project
