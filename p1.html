KRATOS COMBAT ROBOT

MIT Combat Robotics Club – January 2025–Present

What?

  • Created a combat robot to compete in the National Havoc Robot League
  • Fabricated an axial flux motor for increased torque on robot weapon

How?

  • Utilized Fusion360 and Solidworks for CAM design and machining
  • Operated a CNC mill and waterjet to fabricate robot parts
  • Rewired and optimized custom ESC and brushless motor control circuits for reliable operation

Results

  • Kratos produced 9000 lbs. of force in our latest match, destroying our opponent’s frame
  • Successfully built a functional axial flux motor
