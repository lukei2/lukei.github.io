Oreo Combat Robot (WIP)

MIT Combat Robotics Club – January 2025–Present

What?

  • Created a combat robot to compete in the National Havoc Robot League

How?

  • Used Onshape for robot design and center of mass analysis
  • Utilized 3D Printing to manufacture prototype parts
  • Created custom circuitry to interface with the Teensy 5.0 microcontrollers

Results

  • I have not had the opportunity to test Oreo on the field yet, but initial runs are promising!
